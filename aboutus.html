About Us


Mission Statement

Our mission is to find all dogs a loving and nurturing home. This includes dogs on the streets or dogs residing in homes where they aren't given enough attention. Every pup deserves a warm, loving family.


How Adopt-a-Paw Was Founded

Our foundation was founded by Harry Potter and Ronald Weasley back in 1964. The two friends came to realization over their years of working with dogs that dogs deserve and require a lot of care. With that, the two best friends founded this organization which is now operating under the current head: Rodney Le.
